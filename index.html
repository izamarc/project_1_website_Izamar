The Vampire Diaries

Intro

The Vampires Diaries first premiered on September 10,2009 on the CW. The first episode attracted 3.6 million views. This got the most views that any series premeire since 2006 which was when the networkjust started. The show was preduced by Kevin Williamson and Julie Plec. The show is based on a book by L.J.Smith.

Main Characters

Nina Dobrev played Elena Gilbert the main character of the show. Steven R. McQueen who played as Elena's brother, Jeremy Gilbert. Paul Wesley played Stefan Salvator, Elena's first lover. Ian Somerhalder played Elena's second lover and Stefan's evil brother, Damon Salvator. Zach Roerig who played Matt Honeycutt, Elena's ex. Sara Canning played Jenna Sommers, Elena's aunt. Candace King and Kat Graham played as Elena's bestfriends Caroline Forbes and Bonnie Bennet.
