The story begins when Elena (the main character) is getting over the loss of her parents with her brother Jeremy. Her brother is a drugatic. Elena finds out her bestfriend Bonnie Bennett is a witch. All of that and yet discovers that a her crush changes her whole life. Her crush is a 169 year old vampire who has a devil brother,Damon Salvator, a 177 year old vampire.

Season 2 is when Elena finds out about the werewolves and meets the original vampires. And at the end of season 2 Elena's aunt ,Jenna Sommers, dies. Season three is about Klaus and the origin of his family. Then after his story is told it resumes at Elena's eighteenth birthday. Elena is still greiving her aunt as well as her brother Jeremy.
